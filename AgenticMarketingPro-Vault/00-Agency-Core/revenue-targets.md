# Revenue Targets

## 2026 targets

| Metric | Q1 target | Q2 target | Q3 target | Q4 target | FY 2026 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| MRR (managed services) | $15K | $30K | $50K | $75K | $50K avg |
| SaaS MRR (Year 2 launch) | — | — | — | — | — |
| Active clients (managed) | 4 | 7 | 11 | 17 | 17 EOY |
| Avg retainer | $3.75K | $4.3K | $4.5K | $4.4K | $4.3K |
| Gross margin | 55% | 60% | 65% | 68% | 62% |
| Churn (monthly) | 0% | 0% | <5% | <5% | <3% avg |
| NPS | — | — | 50+ | 55+ | 50+ |

## Churn ceiling
- **Hard ceiling:** 5% monthly churn. Above this for 2 consecutive months = emergency founder intervention.
- **Acceptable churn:** <3% monthly (industry benchmark for agencies is 8-12%).
- **No-fault churn** (client acquired, pivoted, ran out of budget): excluded from churn calculation but tracked separately.

## Margin floor
- **Hard floor:** 50% gross margin per client. Below this = client is unprofitable; either renegotiate or fire.
- **Target:** 65%+ at scale (achievable at 15+ clients due to OS cost amortization).

## Revenue concentration rules
- No single client >35% of MRR (founder reviews if breached)
- Top 3 clients <70% of MRR combined
- Top 5 clients <85% of MRR combined

## Pricing power signals (when to raise prices)
- NPS consistently >60 for 2 quarters
- Win rate on pitches >40%
- 3+ clients on waitlist
- When any of the above are true → raise prices for new clients by 15%; existing clients grandfathered for current contract term.

## Quarterly review cadence
- Q1 review: March 31 (validate PMF with first 4 clients)
- Q2 review: June 30 (validate compounding thesis — are KPIs accelerating?)
- Q3 review: Sept 30 (prep for Year 2 SaaS launch)
- Q4 review: Dec 31 (annual planning, set Year 2 targets)
