# Business Context

## Founding story
AgenticMarketingPro was founded in [year] to answer a question traditional agencies couldn't: *what happens to marketing execution when AI agents can do most of the work?* We exist to prove that an agent-native agency can deliver better outcomes, faster, at a fraction of the cost — while keeping humans in control of every high-risk decision.

## Mission
Build the most context-aware marketing operating system on the planet. Use it to compound market visibility for clients faster than any human team could. Pass the savings back to clients as better ROI and to the agency as higher margins.

## Three-year vision
- **Year 1 (current):** Stand up the agent OS, validate with 5-10 clients, hit $50K MRR.
- **Year 2:** Productize the OS as a SaaS for in-house marketing teams; expand to 25 managed clients; hit $250K MRR (services + SaaS).
- **Year 3:** Vertical specialization in 2 industries (B2B SaaS + e-commerce); enterprise tier live; hit $1M ARR.

## What we do
We run the full digital marketing stack — SEO (technical, on-page, off-page, programmatic), AEO/GEO (AI search), paid (Google/Microsoft/Meta/LinkedIn/TikTok/Reddit), social, email/lifecycle, content, CRO, analytics, and revenue intelligence — as an integrated agent OS rather than a human team.

## What we don't do
- White-label work for other agencies (we need direct client relationships to compound the vault)
- Pure execution retainers without strategy input (we sell outcomes, not hours)
- Industries where AI marketing is restricted (e.g., tobacco, weapons, adult)
- Clients with sub-$2K/month budgets (the OS doesn't have unit economics below this)

## Founder's note
> The agent OS is the product. The agency is the wrapper that lets us refine it against real client problems. Never let the agency side compromise the OS side — every shortcut taken for a client is a debt the OS pays back later.
