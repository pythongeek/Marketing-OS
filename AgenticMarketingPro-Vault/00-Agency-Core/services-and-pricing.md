# Services and Pricing

## Service tiers

| Tier | Retainer (USD/mo) | What's included | Best for |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $2,500 | SEO (technical + on-page + GSC), 4 content pieces/mo, weekly digest | Early-stage startups validating PMF |
| **Growth** | $4,500 | Starter + off-page (10 links/mo target), 8 content pieces, AEO/GEO baseline, social calendar | Series A-C SaaS, scaling e-commerce |
| **Scale** | $8,500 | Growth + programmatic SEO (up to 500 pages/mo), paid ads management (up to $30K/mo ad spend), CRO program, email/lifecycle | Growth-stage companies with in-house marketing lead |
| **Enterprise** | $15,000+ | Custom — full stack, dedicated strategist, quarterly QBRs, SSO + audit logs | Series C+ / public companies |

## Add-on services
- **AI search brand monitoring** (Profound + Otterly + manual): $800/mo
- **Influencer/creator management**: $1,500/mo + creator fees
- **Crisis monitoring** (24/7 reputation): $1,000/mo
- **Quarterly deep audits** (technical + content + competitive): $2,500 one-time

## Pricing principles
1. **Never discount retainers.** Discount is a one-time credit, not a permanent price cut.
2. **Always charge for ad spend management** as % of spend (10-15%) — never flat fee.
3. **Pricing reviews happen annually**, not mid-contract.
4. **Custom quotes** require founder approval — no strategist has authority to discount.

## Onboarding fees
- Starter / Growth: $0 (bundled into first month)
- Scale: $2,500 one-time (covers full audit + 90-day strategy)
- Enterprise: $5,000 one-time (covers audit + strategy + integration setup)

## Contract terms
- 3-month minimum on all retainers (agency needs 90 days to show compounding results)
- 30-day cancellation notice after minimum term
- All work product stored in client's vault folder remains agency IP unless contract specifies otherwise
