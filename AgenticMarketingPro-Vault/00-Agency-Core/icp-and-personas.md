# Ideal Client Profiles & Personas

## ICP 1: Series A-C B2B SaaS
- **Company size:** 30-300 employees
- **ARR:** $2M-$30M
- **Marketing team:** 1-3 in-house marketers (often a Head of Marketing + 1 IC)
- **Pain:** Has product-market fit, but marketing isn't compounding. Hires don't move the needle. SEO feels like a black box. AI search is changing the rules.
- **Why we win:** Our agent OS gives them the equivalent of a 5-person team for the cost of 1 mid-level hire.
- **Pricing tier:** Growth or Scale ($4,500-$8,500/mo)
- **Decision-maker persona:** VP Marketing or Head of Growth, 32-45, reports to CEO/CMO, has been burned by agencies before.

## ICP 2: Scaling e-commerce brands (DTC)
- **Company size:** 15-100 employees
- **Revenue:** $3M-$25M GMV
- **Marketing team:** Founder/CEO + 1-2 performance marketers
- **Pain:** Paid social ROAS declining. Organic search underinvested. Email list underutilized. AI engines aren't citing them.
- **Why we win:** We cover organic + paid + email + AEO as an integrated system, not 4 disconnected vendors.
- **Pricing tier:** Growth or Scale ($4,500-$8,500/mo)
- **Decision-maker persona:** Founder/CEO or Head of Ecommerce, 30-50, leans performance-marketing, skeptical of "brand" spend.

## ICP 3: Multi-location service businesses
- **Company size:** 50-500 employees, 5-50 locations
- **Pain:** Local SEO is a mess across locations. GBP management is manual. Reviews going unanswered. No consistent visibility in geo-grid.
- **Why we win:** Programmatic local SEO at scale + automated review responses.
- **Pricing tier:** Scale ($8,500/mo) or Enterprise ($15,000+)
- **Decision-maker persona:** VP Operations or Director of Marketing, 35-55, pragmatic, ROI-focused.

## ICP 4: Series C+ / Public enterprise (Year 2+)
- **Company size:** 500+ employees
- **Pain:** Marketing ops team is overloaded. Compliance requirements block most AI tools. SOC 2 needed.
- **Why we win:** Enterprise tier with SSO, audit logs, data residency, dedicated strategist.
- **Pricing tier:** Enterprise ($15,000+/mo)
- **Decision-maker persona:** Director/VP of Marketing Operations, 35-50, risk-averse, values process.

## Anti-ICP (who we don't take)
- Pre-revenue startups (no budget for 90-day compounding cycle)
- Agencies looking for white-label partner (we need direct client relationship)
- Crypto/gambling/tobacco/adult industries (compliance + brand risk)
- Companies that want "quick wins" or "viral campaigns" (we do compounding visibility, not stunts)
- Clients who refuse to share analytics/ad account access (we need data to operate)
