## Severity definitions
- **S1 (Critical):** Client revenue at risk or data breach. Founder paged. SLA: 1h response.
- **S2 (High):** KPI materially affected. Strategist paged. SLA: 4h response.
- **S3 (Medium):** Degraded performance. Logged for daily review.
- **S4 (Low):** Cosmetic or edge case. Logged for weekly review.
