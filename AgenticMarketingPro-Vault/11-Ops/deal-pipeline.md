# Deal Pipeline

> Prospects surfaced by Revenue Scout Agent + status through pipeline.

## Pipeline stages
1. **Identified:** Scout found trigger event
2. **Enriched:** Scout enriched with company data + ICP match score
3. **Qualified:** Human bizdev reviewed + approved for outreach
4. **Outreach sent:** Cold email sequence started
5. **Meeting booked:** Prospect agreed to discovery call
6. **Pitch delivered:** Custom proposal sent
7. **Negotiating:** Terms under discussion
8. **Closed-won:** Contract signed
9. **Closed-lost:** Prospect declined (with reason)
