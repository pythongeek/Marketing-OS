# Topic Clusters

> Topical authority maps per industry vertical. Owned by Topic Expert Agent.

## How clusters work
Each cluster has:
- 1 pillar page (broad topic, high volume)
- 5-15 sub-topic pages (specific angles, linked to pillar)
- Internal linking: every sub-topic links to pillar; pillar links to every sub-topic
- Schema: pillar uses Article, sub-topics use Article + mentions pillar via `about`
