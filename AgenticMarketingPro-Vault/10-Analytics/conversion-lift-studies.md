# Conversion Lift Studies

> Pre/post analysis of every major change (test winner rollout, new page launch, etc.).

## Methodology
- **Pre/post window:** 14 days before, 14 days after
- **Confidence interval:** 95% required to declare significant lift
- **Seasonality control:** compare to same period last year if available
- **Confounding variables:** document any other changes during window

## Common lift studies we run
- New pillar article published → organic traffic to category
- Schema deployment → rich result CTR
- CTA change on landing page → conversion rate
- Email flow redesign → revenue per email
- AEO content optimization → citation rate
