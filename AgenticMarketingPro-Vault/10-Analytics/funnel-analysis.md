# Funnel Analysis

> Per-client conversion funnel with leakage points. Updated monthly by Analytics Expert + CRO Agent.

## Funnel definition (B2B SaaS template)
1. Visitor (any session)
2. Engaged session (GA4: >10s, >1 pageview, or conversion)
3. Content consumer (visited pillar/blog)
4. Lead magnet downloader (gated content)
5. MQL (Marketing Qualified Lead — scored in HubSpot)
6. SQL (Sales Qualified Lead — accepted by sales)
7. Opportunity (opportunity created in CRM)
8. Closed-won
