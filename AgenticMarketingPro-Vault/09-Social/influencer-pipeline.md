## Vetting checklist (per creator)
- [ ] Audience demographics match client ICP
- [ ] Engagement rate ≥ industry benchmark
- [ ] Fake follower check passed (HypeAuditor: <15% fake)
- [ ] Brand safety: no controversial past content
- [ ] Past performance: ≥3 case studies with measurable results
- [ ] Pricing fits client budget
- [ ] Contract template approved by Compliance Agent

## Creator brief template
1. Product/service overview (1 paragraph)
2. Key messages (3 bullets)
3. Forbidden claims (list)
4. CTA + tracking link
5. Creative freedom (what they can/can't change)
6. Disclosure requirements (#ad, paid partnership tag)
7. Delivery timeline
8. Performance tracking (UTM, affiliate link, promo code)
