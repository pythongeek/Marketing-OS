### Keyword Priority Scoring Framework

For every target keyword, score on three axes (1-5 each, max 15):

- **R** = Relevance to AgenticMarketingPro's positioning (5 = directly product-aligned)
- **I** = Search Intent match (5 = BOFU/buyer-ready)
- **V** = Volume potential (5 = 10K+/month)

**Priority Tier 1 (Score 13-15):** Map to pillar pages and BOFU cluster posts.
**Priority Tier 2 (Score 10-12):** Map to MOFU cluster posts.
**Priority Tier 3 (Score 7-9):** Map to TOFU posts and programmatic pages.
**Priority Tier 4 (<7):** Deprioritize or deindex.
