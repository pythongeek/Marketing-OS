# LinkedIn Lead Generation Strategy
## AgenticMarketingPro — Founder-Led Growth Engine

> **Strategic Thesis:** The founder's voice IS the moat. In an emerging category like agentic marketing, buyers don't trust vendor claims — they trust founder POV. This strategy weaponizes the founder's profile to (1) generate inbound demand via thought leadership and (2) convert that demand via a disciplined 5-touch outreach motion backed by Sales Navigator precision targeting.

---

## SECTION 1: Founder Daily Posting Schedule (Mon–Sat)

**Cadence:** 1 post/day, 6 days/week = **~26 posts/month**. Sunday is dark for recovery and engagement-only mode.

| Day | Theme | Format | CTA Style | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | **Tactical Playbook** — "Here's exactly how to do X" | Carousel (PDF, 6–10 slides) or text post with framework | "Save this for your team meeting" | Saves + Profile Visits |
| **Tuesday** | **Industry Signal** — Commentary on news, funding, new AI tools | Short text post (150–250 words) with hot take | "Agree or disagree? Reply below" | Comments + DMs |
| **Wednesday** | **Founder POV / Personal Story** — Lessons, failures, behind-the-scenes | Story-style text post or 60-sec selfie video | "DM me if this resonates" | DMs + Connection Requests |
| **Thursday** | **Case Study / Customer Win** — Specific numbers, before/after | Text post with screenshot/graphic proof | "Want the playbook? Comment 'PLAYBOOK'" | Comments + Lead Capture |
| **Friday** | **Contrarian / Hot Take** — Disagree with conventional wisdom | Punchy text post or poll | "Change my mind" | Engagement Velocity + Reach |
| **Saturday** | **Soft Sell / Mission** — Why we built AgenticMarketingPro, hiring, culture | Long-form text or photo essay | "Building something here — let's connect Monday" | Top-of-funnel Connections |

**Weekly Time Block:** Sunday evening (60 min) — batch script 6 posts. Daily engagement window: 7:30–8:00 AM and 5:30–6:00 PM in target audience's timezone (ET default).

## SECTION 2: 5-Touch Outreach Sequence

**Pre-requisite:** Lead has engaged with content (liked, commented, or visited profile) OR matched Sales Navigator ICP filter. Always check engagement history before Touch 1.

| Touch | Day | Channel | Message | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1 — Connection** | Day 0 | Connection Request | 250-character personalized note referencing a *specific* post they engaged with or a shared experience. **No pitch.** | Establish presence in network |
| **2 — Value Drop** | Day 3–4 | DM after acceptance | Share 1 piece of high-value content (case study, framework PDF, or relevant article) with a 2-sentence observation tied to *their* company/role | Build reciprocity, demonstrate expertise |
| **3 — Discovery** | Day 8–10 | DM | Insight-based question: *"Saw [Company] just [specific event]. Curious how you're thinking about [challenge] given [trend]."* **No pitch.** | Uncover pain, start conversation |
| **4 — Demo / Soft Offer** | Day 14–17 | DM + Email combo | "Worth 20 min?" with a *specific* outcome promised (not feature dump). Reference the conversation arc from Touch 3. | Convert conversation to meeting |
| **5 — Breakup / Close** | Day 21–24 | DM + Email | Polite breakup: *"Closing the loop — not sure timing works, but if [specific outcome] becomes a priority in Q[X], I'd love to revisit."* | Recapture attention OR trigger referral |

**Critical Rules:**
- **Never pitch before Touch 3.** Connection + value are pure give.
- **Personalization tax:** Every message references something specific. No templates that read as templates.
- **Multi-channel:** DM + email after Touch 3 to increase surface area.
- **CRM logging:** Every touch, response, and sentiment logged. If no engagement after Touch 5, recycle to 90-day nurture.

### D. Voice & Tone Guidelines

- **Sentence length:** Mix of 3-word punchy and 25-word substantive. No academic prose.
- **First person:** Heavy. "I" statements. "We" for product. Never "the company."
- **Cynicism allowance:** 20%. You're allowed to call out bad takes and call out vendors. CMOs respect directness.
- **No emojis** in professional posts (use sparingly for hooks).
- **Always end with a question or CTA.**

## SECTION 7: LinkedIn Ad Retargeting Strategy

### A. Audience Stack (Built Sequentially)

| Funnel Stage | Audience Source | Ad Objective | Creative |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Cold (Top)** | Lookalike of CMO title profiles, Job change audience, "AI Marketing" interest | Video views / single image | 30-sec founder manifesto video + "Is your marketing team agentic?" carousel |
| **Mid (Engaged)** | 30-day post engagers, profile visitors, video viewers (50%+) | Conversion (lead gen form) | Lead magnet ad: "The CMO's Agentic Marketing Readiness Audit" |
| **Hot (Bottom)** | Lead form openers, website visitors (via Insight tag), case study downloaders | Conversion (demo request) | Customer testimonial video + "Book a 20-min demo" CTA |

### B. Creative Rotation Rules

- **Refresh creative every 14 days** to avoid ad fatigue.
- **A/B test 3 variables per cycle:** Hook (first 3 seconds / first line), format (video vs. carousel vs. single image), CTA (DM vs. download vs. demo).
- **3-2-1 budget rule:** 3 ad sets running, 2 audiences each, 1 winning creative scaling.

### C. Lead Form vs. Landing Page

| Use Lead Form When | Use Landing Page When |
|---|---|
| Top-of-funnel asset (audit, playbook) | Mid-funnel demo request, pricing |
| Mobile-heavy audience | Need qualification questions |
| Speed-to-lead critical (under 5 min) | Want to track full funnel + pixel events |

### D. Spend Allocation (Suggested Monthly Starting Budget — $5K/mo)

- 40% Cold (awareness + audience building)
- 35% Mid-funnel (lead magnet conversion)
- 25% Hot (demo request / bottom funnel)

### E. Retargeting Sequence (After Conversion)

1. **Day 0:** Lead form submission → instant deliver asset + add to CRM.
2. **Day 1:** Founder video DM (personalized).
3. **Day 3:** Email nurture starts.
4. **Day 7:** Retargeted ad: Case study of similar company.
5. **Day 14:** Retargeted ad: Invitation to monthly CMO roundtable.
6. **Day 30:** Move to long-term nurture if unqualified for direct sales.
