# 1. 🎯 STRATEGIC FOUNDATION

## Brand Positioning Statement

> *"AgenticMarketingPro is the inside look at how modern B2B SaaS teams build, deploy, and scale AI marketing agents — told by a founder who's doing it in public."*

## The Three Content Pillars

Every piece of content must map to ≥1 pillar:

| Pillar | Description | % of Content | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🧠 **Tactical Playbooks** | Step-by-step how-tos, frameworks, templates | 40% | Save/share, SEO, lead gen |
| 🔬 **Build in Public** | Founder journey, metrics, wins, failures, decisions | 35% | Trust, community, virality |
| ⚡ **Industry POV** | Hot takes, news commentary, predictions, contrarian views | 25% | Engagement, reach, thought leadership |

## Founder-Led Content Architecture

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│         FOUNDER (Primary Voice: 70%)        │
│  Opinions • Stories • Frameworks • BTS      │
└──────────────┬──────────────────────────────┘
               │
       ┌───────┴────────┐
       ▼                ▼
┌──────────────┐  ┌─────────────────┐
│   TEAM (20%) │  │ BRAND FACELESS  │
│  Case studies│  │  Carousels/AI   │
│  Tutorials   │  │  Templates (10%)│
└──────────────┘  └─────────────────┘
```

## Voice Principles

- **Specific over generic** — "We hit $14K MRR with 1 blog post" beats "How to grow your SaaS"
- **Show receipts** — Screenshots, dashboards, real numbers
- **Tactical density** — Every post should have ≥1 actionable takeaway
- **Low ego, high signal** — No bro-marketing. Founder-to-founder respect.
- **Hook in 3 seconds** — Treat every post like a cold open.

# 2. 🐦 X / TWITTER PLAYBOOK

## Posting Cadence: 3-5 Posts/Day

```
Daily Mix (M-F):
├── 1 Single-point tweet (high-frequency)
├── 1 Thread (Tue/Thu) or Quote RT chain
├── 1 Engagement tweet (poll, question, or quote tweet)
├── 1 Reply/quote to bigger account
└── 1 Founder observation / BTS
```

## The 5 Daily Post Types (Rotating)

### Type 1: ⚡ The Atomic Insight Tweet
**Format:** Hook → Micro-lesson → CTA
**Example:**
> Most B2B SaaS founders burn $5K/mo on ads before they ever test one distribution channel that costs $0.
>
> Try this instead:
> → Pick one platform where your buyer already hangs out
> → Post daily for 90 days
> → Track DMs, not likes
>
> Distribution beats product. Always.

**Specs:** 200-280 chars | 1-2 emojis max | No hashtags (kills reach in 2026)

### Type 2: 🧵 The Thread (2x/week: Tue & Thu)
**Structure:**
1. **Hook tweet** — Contrarian, specific, or pattern-interrupt
2. **Context** — Why this matters NOW
3. **Framework** — Step-by-step breakdown (5-7 steps)
4. **Proof** — Screenshot, data, or example
5. **Tactical takeaway** — "Do this Monday"
6. **CTA** — Follow + RT last tweet

**Thread Topic Bank (rotate):**
- "How we [X] without [Y]"
- "The [X] frameworks I use for [Y]"
- "[Number] lessons from [milestone]"
- "Steal this [template/script/framework]"
- "Why we're [doing X differently]"

### Type 3: 💬 Engagement Tweet
**Format variations:**
- **Poll:** "What's your biggest bottleneck with AI marketing agents?"
- **This or That:** "Build in public / Stay stealth?"
- **Question:** "Drop your SaaS below. I'll roast your landing page (nicely)."
- **Fill in blank:** "The best $100/mo I spend on my SaaS is _____"

### Type 4: 🔁 Quote Tweet / Reply
Target 2-3 tweets/day from:
- Larger accounts in your niche (indie hackers, SaaS founders)
- Industry news accounts
- Customer tweets
- Competitor insights (with better take)

**Reply formula:**
> "[Specific acknowledgment] + [Your unique angle] + [Tactical add]"

### Type 5: 🪞 Build in Public
Weekly examples:
- Revenue screenshot (Mon morning)
- Tool stack update
- "What I got wrong about X"
- Hiring update
- Customer win with specifics

## X Growth Tactics

1. **The 30-Minute Engagement Block** — Reply to 10-15 relevant tweets every morning before posting your own
2. **Reply-to-Win** — When someone tweets about a problem you solve, reply with a full mini-solution (not "DM me")
3. **Thread Recycling** — Top-performing threads become blog posts, YouTube videos, carousel posts
4. **Spaces Monthly** — Host/co-host 1 X Space/month with 2-3 founder guests
5. **Pin Strategy** — Always pin your best thread or current offer
6. **DM Funnel** — Anyone who engages 3+ times in a week gets a personalized DM

## X KPIs
| Metric | Target (90 days) |
|---|---|
| Impressions/tweet | 1,000+ |
| Engagement rate | 2%+ |
| Profile visits/day | 100+ |
| Follower growth | +500-1,000/mo |
| Threads going viral (>10K views) | 2-4/mo |

# 3. 📺 YOUTUBE PLAYBOOK

## Content Architecture: Weekly Upload

```
WEEKLY YOUTUBE SCHEDULE
├── Monday:    Long-form video publishes (8-15 min)
├── Wednesday: 1 YouTube Short from week's footage
├── Friday:    Community post (poll/question)
└── Saturday:  1 YouTube Short (repurposed clip)
```

## The 6 Video Formats (Rotate)

| Format | Length | Frequency | Example Title |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🎓 **Tactical Tutorial** | 10-15 min | 2x/mo | "How to Build an AI Lead Gen Agent in 20 min" |
| 🔬 **Build in Public Update** | 8-12 min | 1x/mo | "Our SaaS Month 6: $X MRR, What Worked, What Didn't" |
| 📚 **Founder Case Study** | 12-15 min | 1x/mo | "I Audited 10 SaaS Landing Pages. Here's What I Found." |
| 🧠 **Framework Video** | 10-12 min | 2x/mo | "The 5-Step AI Marketing System We Use at AgenticMarketingPro" |

| 💬 **Founder Q&A** | 10-15 min | 1x/mo | "Answering Your Top 10 SaaS Marketing Questions" |

## YouTube SEO System

### Title Formula
> `[Specific Number] + [Specific Outcome] + [Timeframe/Constraint]`

**Examples:**
- ❌ "How to Market Your SaaS"
- ✅ "I Spent $5K on SaaS Marketing. Here's What Worked (and What Didn't)."

### Description Template
```
Hook (1-2 sentences with keyword) →
Timestamps (Chapters) →
Key links & resources →
About AgenticMarketingPro (3 sentences) →
Social links →
#hashtags (3-5 max)
```

### Tags Strategy
- 1 broad keyword (e.g., "B2B marketing")
- 3-5 long-tail keywords (e.g., "AI marketing agents for SaaS")
- 2 competitor alternatives (use ethically)
- 1 branded tag

### Thumbnail System

**The 3-Layer Thumbnail:**
```
┌────────────────────────────┐
│                            │
│   [EXPRESSIVE FACE]   [BIG NUMBER/SYMBOL]  │
│   (founder face)      (e.g., $47K or 🔥)  │
│                            │
│   [3-5 WORD HOOK TEXT]    │
│   (yellow/red, bold)      │
│                            │
└────────────────────────────┘
```

**Thumbnail Rules:**
- Face with emotion (3 thumb sizes visible)
- Max 5 words of text
- High contrast (yellow, red, white)
- Never use stock photos
- A/B test using YouTube's built-in tool

## The First 30 Seconds (Retention Hook)

```
SECOND 0-3:    Pattern interrupt (movement, sound, bold claim)
SECOND 3-10:   "In this video, I'll show you [3 specific things]"
SECOND 10-30:  Cold open — preview of the BEST moment of the video
SECOND 30+:    "But first, context..."
```

## YouTube Growth Tactics

1. **Shorts Funnel** — Every long-form video generates 3-5 Shorts that link back
2. **Comment Mining** — Respond to every comment in first 2 hours (algorithm boost)
3. **End Screen CTA** — Always have 2 videos + 1 subscribe button
4. **Collaboration** — 1 collab video per month with similar-sized creator
5. **Premiere Strategy** — Use YouTube Premieres for big videos to drive live engagement
6. **Email Embed** — Embed YouTube videos in every newsletter (signals to algorithm)

## YouTube KPIs
| Metric | Target (90 days) |
|---|---|
| Avg views/video | 1,000+ |
| CTR | 5-10% |
| Avg view duration | 50%+ of video |
| Subscriber growth | +300-500/mo |
| Videos hitting 10K views | 1-2/mo |

# 4. 🎵 TIKTOK PLAYBOOK

## Posting Cadence: 1 Daily Short-Form Video

**Why daily for B2B:** TikTok's algorithm rewards consistency more than perfection. Daily posting compounds.

## The 7 TikTok Content Buckets

| Bucket | Example | % of Content |
|---|---|---|
| 🧠 **Quick Tutorial** (30-60s) | "3 AI prompts every B2B founder needs" | 25% |
| 🤔 **Contrarian Take** (15-30s) | "Cold email is dead. Here's what replaced it." | 20% |
| 📊 **Show Your Screen** (30-60s) | Walk through a dashboard, tool, or workflow | 20% |
| 🪞 **Founder POV** (15-30s) | Hot mic moments, "day in my life," workspace tours | 15% |
| 🎯 **Trend-Jacking** (15-30s) | Use trending sound, adapt to B2B context | 10% |
| 📚 **Carousel Reading** (30-45s) | Static image + voiceover explaining it | 5% |
| 🎤 **Green Screen Reaction** (30-60s) | React to industry news, competitor content | 5% |

## The TikTok Hook Stack

**First 1.5 seconds MUST accomplish:**
1. Stop the scroll (movement, sound, bold text)
2. Promise value (specific outcome)
3. Pattern interrupt (unusual camera angle, glitch effect)

**The "3-Second Promise" Framework:**
> "I'm about to show you [X], so you can [Y], without [Z]."

## TikTok Trend-Jacking Protocol

```
STEP 1: Daily 10-min trend scan (foryou + explore)
STEP 2: Filter by sound that's <7 days old
STEP 3: Brainstorm "How does this apply to B2B SaaS?"
STEP 4: Film in <30 min using existing scripts
STEP 5: Post within 24 hours of trend peak
```

## TikTok Production Workflow (20 min/video)

| Step | Time | Tool |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Write hook + outline | 5 min | Notes app |
| 2. Set up phone + lighting | 2 min | Ring light, phone tripod |
| 3. Record 2-3 takes | 5 min | iPhone/Android |
| 4. Edit in CapCut | 5 min | Auto-captions, jump cuts |
| 5. Add CTA + post | 3 min | Native posting |
