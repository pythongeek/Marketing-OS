## Key Success Principles

1. **Quality over quantity** - One DR 70 link > ten DR 20 links
2. **Relevance is non-negotiable** - Marketing context links only
3. **Diversify anchor text** - 70% branded/URL, 20% generic, 10% exact match
4. **Never pay for "DA 30+" packages** - Avoid PBNs, link farms, Fiverr gigs
5. **Reputation matters** - Check referring domains for spam score monthly
6. **Compound effect** - Months 7-12 will be 3-5x more productive than months 1-3 as authority builds
7. **Track everything** - You can't scale what you don't measure
