## 3. Digital PR Campaign Ideas

Digital PR generates **top-tier media links** (DA 70-90) that move the needle fastest. Focus on **newsworthy angles**, not promotional pitches.

### Campaign 1: "The Agentic AI Marketing Index" (Monthly Press Drops)

**Pitch:** Monthly data release on AI agent adoption in marketing—sent to 50-100 journalists under embargo.

**Target outlets:** TechCrunch, VentureBeat, Forbes, Inc., Marketing Week, AdWeek, The Drum, Digiday

**Asset:** Press release + interactive dashboard + 3-5 chart images + expert commentary

**Expected yield:** 5-15 mentions per drop

### Campaign 2: "AI vs Human Marketer" Experiment

**Pitch:** Run a public, multi-month experiment where AgenticMarketingPro's AI agents compete against human marketers on identical campaigns (SEO, paid ads, social). Publish full results.

**Hook:** Journalists LOVE competitive experiments. Think "Man vs. Machine 2.0."

**Target outlets:** Wired, Fast Company, AdAge, The Verge, HBR online, NY Times

### Campaign 3: "The $1M Marketing Budget Bot"

**Story:** Build an AI agent, give it $1M of (simulated) marketing budget, document the journey in real-time on a public dashboard.

**Hook:** Transparency + stakes + narrative arc = perfect PR story.

### Campaign 4: "Marketing Agency Layoff Index"

**Newsjacking play:** Track and publish data on marketing layoffs vs. AI agent adoption. Provides ongoing commentary hook for journalists writing about industry changes.

### Campaign 5: Annual Industry Awards

**"The Agentic AI Marketing Awards"** — Free awards for companies using AI agents innovatively. Winners get a badge, free PR, and a backlink to their profile page (which links back to you).
