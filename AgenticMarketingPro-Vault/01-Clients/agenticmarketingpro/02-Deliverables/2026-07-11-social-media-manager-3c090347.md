## 4. LinkedIn Ads Strategy

### Campaign Architecture (3-Layer Funnel)

#### 🎯 Layer 1: TOFU — Awareness
- **Objective:** Brand awareness / Video views
- **Budget:** 20% of ad spend
- **Targeting:**
  - Job titles: CMO, VP Marketing, Marketing Director, Founder, CEO
  - Company size: 11-1000 employees
  - Industries: SaaS, Fintech, E-commerce, B2B Services
  - Skills: Marketing Strategy, Digital Marketing, Marketing Automation
  - Groups: Marketing leaders groups (3-5 targeted)
- **Creative:**
  - 15s vertical video: "What if your marketing team was 3x the size?"
  - Single image: Bold stat overlay (e.g., "73% less time on content production")
  - Document ad: Lead magnet preview (first 3 slides)

#### 🔄 Layer 2: MOFU — Consideration
- **Objective:** Lead generation (Lead Gen Forms)
- **Budget:** 50% of ad spend
- **Targeting:** Retarget TOFU engagers + Lookalikes (1-3%)
- **Lead Magnets:**
  1. *"The CMO's Guide to Agentic AI Marketing"* (PDF, 35 pages)
  2. *"AI Marketing Agent ROI Calculator"* (interactive tool)
  3. *"Free Marketing Ops Audit"* (gated)
- **Creative:**
  - Carousel: 5 use cases, one per slide
  - Video testimonial (45s): client case study
  - Conversation Ad: "Which marketing task would you automate first? A/B/C/D"

#### 🔥 Layer 3: BOFU — Conversion
- **Objective:** Conversions / Demo requests
- **Budget:** 30% of ad spend
- **Targeting:** 
  - Retarget MOFU leads who didn't convert
  - Company lists (Account-Based Marketing)
  - Website retargeting (matched audiences)
- **Creative:**
  - Single image: "Book a 20-min demo" with client logos
  - Document ad: One-page case study with CTA
  - Sponsored InMail: Personal invitation to demo or event

### Suggested Monthly Ad Budget Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Budget | Expected Leads |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| Starter | $3,000 | 30-50 |
| Growth | $8,000 | 80-130 |
| Scale | $20,000+ | 200-350 |

### Ad Creative Best Practices
- [ ] Lead Gen Forms ≤ 4 fields (Name, Email, Company, Role)
- [ ] Hook in first 3 seconds of video
- [ ] Mobile-first design (80% of LinkedIn feed is mobile)
- [ ] A/B test 3-5 creatives per campaign before scaling
- [ ] Refresh creatives every 14-21 days (fatigue)
